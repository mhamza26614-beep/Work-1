Summarize this story: One afternoon, a curious boy named Alex was bored after finishing his homework. Instead of watching TV, he decided to try something new. He set a rule for himself: “I will learn or create something in just 30 minutes.”
On the first day, he spent 30 minutes learning how to bake a cake.
On the second day, he used 30 minutes to learn some French.
On the third day, he used 30 minutes how to build a structure of Dam.
Days passed, and Alex kept doing his 30-minute idea every day.
After one month, something amazing happened:
He could bake tasty cakes.
His French had grown a lot.
He could build small creative models from simple things at home.
His teacher noticed his creativity and asked, “Alex, how did you learn so many things so quickly?”
Alex smiled and said,
“I just used 30 minutes every day.”
The teacher told the class, “Small effort every day can create big success.”
